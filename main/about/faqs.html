Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)!

How long does the process take?

The processing time varies depending on the order. Everyone's needs are different and we try to do our best to accommodate them. On average, it takes about 2-4 weeks from start to finish.

Can I save on shipping costs if I pick up my object or object's in person?

Absolutely! Just contact us before we ship your order and let us know that you'll be picking it up in person. You can pick up your object at our UWSP production facility.

Do you allow bulk orders?

Due to our limited number of printers and the slow nature of FDM 3D printing, we are unable to accommodate bulk orders at this time, sorry!

Can I reduce the cost of my order if I provide you with pre-made 3D models to print?

Yes. Our 3D modelling services demand an extra charge, so if you already have a 3D model that you'd like printed, just include it in your order request form.

Will different colors, sizes, and materials effect the price of my order?

We are happy to say that we can offer every color for the same price, however, certain printing materials may require a premium. We don't charge extra for larger objects, but we do charge for total material printed so larger objects may use more material.

What if I want my object printed with more than one material or color?

We are able to use our dual-nozzle printers to print with two colors or two similar melting-point filaments at the same time! This type of order is usually special and entails an additional small fee.

If I order multiple objects, can I have each object printed in a different size, color, or material?

Yes, however, not in the same order. We've found that it's much simpler for everyone if each object in the order is identical. If you'd like to order multiple sizes, colors, or materials, then please make separate orders. We will work with you to ship everything together to save on delivery costs.

What if I'm not satisfied with your rendition of my 3D model request?

Our 3D modellers do their best to work with you and bring your ideas to fruition. We ask for as much details as you're able to give us for creating your 3D model. Sometimes our designers must fill in the gaps when details are lacking. If you're not satisfied with the outcome of your 3D model, we offer one free revision based on your feedback.

Do you only create decorative objects or artwork?

Nope! We also design and 3D print fully functional items that you can use for repairs, replacements, or everyday tasks. Many of our objects are both useful and stylish!
